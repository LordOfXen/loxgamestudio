About Us

Who are we?

Lox Game Studios was firstly found in Turkey, Hatay. Even though most of the official work is being done in Washington and California, we currently have 44 employees from many countries such as Turkey, Russia, Sweden, United States, Germany and more without any restrictions about workplace since we prefer working with special cooperation softwares within the studio itself.

As of now, we have done projects such as making API's and bots for Discord, creating libraries for software developers, game development and modding.
Besides all of that, we also try to help small game studios grow bigger by supporting them.
